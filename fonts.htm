Download Greek Fonts
Sth selida ayth 8a breite ellhnikes grammatoseires dia8esimes gia download ka8ws kai odhgies egatastashs wste na mporeite na diabasete ellhnika mesa apo ton browser sas. Ean h akoloy8h le3h : "Ελληνικά" emfanizetai sta ellhnika tote mporeite na agnohsete th selida ayth kai na pate sta periexomena. Se diaforetikh periptwsh akopoy8hste tis odhgies analoga me to leitoyrgiko sas systhma.

1.Windows 3.x
Ean xrhsimopoieite MS-DOS me Windows 3.x tote prepei na kanete ta e3hs : Arxika kante klik EDW gia na "ketebasete" to arxeio "fonts.zip" (60K) pou periexei tis 2 grammatoseires poy xreiazontai kai aposympieste to arxeio ayto se ena proswrino ypokatalogo (p.x. c:\temp). Tora pigainte ston Program Manager kai anoi3te to group "Main" (kanontas diplo klik panw toy) kai stin synexeia ton Pinaka Elegxoy (Control Panel), me ton idio tropo. Mesa ston Pinaka Elegxoy kante diplo klik sto eikonidio "Fonts" kai 8a emfanistei ena para8yro. Pathste twra to koympi "Add" kai 8a emfanistei ena neo para8yro. Sto plaisio "Directory" epile3te ton ypokatalogo ston opoio aposympiesate tis grammatoseires. Ean ola pigan kala stin lista "Fonts" 8a emfanistoyn oi grammatoseires "Arial Greek" kai "Courier New Greek". Epile3te "Copy Fonts To Windows Directory" kai "Select All". Telos pathste "OK" kai teliwsate. Twra to mono poy menei einai na ry8misete ton browser sas. Ayto, an exete Netscape Naviagtor, mporei na ginei ws e3hs : Epile3te diadoxika Options / Preferences kai sth synexeia "Fonts and Colors". Epile3te "Arial Greek" gia Proportional Font kai "Courier New Greek" gia Fixed Font. Pathste "OK" kai teliwsate. Gia perissoteres plhrofories episkefteite thn sxetikh selida sto Panepisthmio Krhths h steilte moy e-mail sth diey8ynsh "panatha@beryl.kapatel.gr".


2.Windows 95
Ean xrhsimopoieite Windows 95 tote prepei na kanete ta e3hs : Arxika kante klik EDW gia na "ketebasete" to arxeio "fonts.zip" (60K) pou periexei tis 2 grammatoseires poy xreiazontai kai aposympieste to arxeio ayto se ena proswrino ypokatalogo (p.x. c:\temp). Ston ypokatalogo ayto tha prepei na yparxoyn ta arxeia "arial.tt_" kai "cour.tt_". Anoi3te ton Control Panel kai kante diplo klik sto eikonidio "Fonts". An ola phgan kala o folder "Fonts" 8a anoi3ei. Kante ta 2 arxeia drag and drop ston folder ayto kai teliwsate, oi grammatoseires 8a egatasta8oyn aytomata. Twra to mono poy menei einai na ry8misete ton browser sas. Parakatw yparxoyn odhgies egatastashs gia Netscape Navigator kai Internet Explorer :

    Netscape Navigator
    Anoi3te to programma kai epile3te diadoxika Options / General Preferences kai sth synexeia "Fonts". Epile3te encoding "Latin1" kai epile3te "Arial Greek" gia proportional font kai "Courier New Greek" gia fixed font. Pathste "OK" kai telos epile3te diadoxika Options / Document Encoding / Western (Latin1)

    Internet Explorer
    Anoi3te to programma kai epile3te diadoxika View / Options kai sth synexeia "Appearance". Epile3te "Arial Greek" gia proportional font kai "Courier New Greek" gia fixed-width font. Telos pathste "OK"

Gia perissoteres plhrofories episkefteite thn sxetikh selida sto Panepisthmio Krhths h steilte moy e-mail sth diey8ynsh "panatha@beryl.kapatel.gr".
